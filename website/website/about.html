About Atlantiplex & Seraphonix Studio

Atlantiplex

Atlantiplex is the underwater innovation platform that blends AI with robotics to transform the deep sea into a living laboratory for AI-powered solutions. Our mission is to bring transparent, scalable AI to complex underwater environments while maintaining a distinct, neon-forward aesthetic inspired by cyberpunk rituals.

Seraphonix Studio Development Team

The Seraphonix Studio development team designs flagship AI tools and underwater control systems. Each member contributes expertise in AI research, software architecture, hardware integration, and UX for immersive operator interfaces. We pursue excellence in design, performance, and reliability, delivering products that fuse science with art.
