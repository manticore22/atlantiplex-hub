## Typography

### Headlines
- **Font**: Playfair Display or similar (elegant, mystical)
- **Weight**: Bold (700)
- **Color**: Gold or Deep Teal
- **Example**: "Atlantiplex Studio - Matrix Broadcasting Platform"

### Body Text
- **Font**: Inter or Segoe UI (modern, readable)
- **Weight**: Regular (400)
- **Color**: Dark Teal or Black
- **Size**: 14-16px

### Accent Text
- **Font**: Orbitron or similar (futuristic)
- **Weight**: Medium (500)
- **Color**: Gold
- **Usage**: Features, highlights, CTAs

## Logo Usage

### Full Logo (Preferred)
- Use when space allows
- Minimum size: 200px width
- Surrounded by clear space equal to inner circle diameter

### Icon Only
- Use at 64px minimum
- Central eye symbol
- Suitable for favicons, app icons

### Wordmark
- "Atlantiplex Studio" in Playfair Display
- Gold with teal accent underline
- Minimum width: 150px

### Combinations
- Logo + Wordmark: Full branding, hero sections
- Logo + Text: Navigation, headers
- Icon Only: Small spaces, favicons, badges

## Do's & Don'ts

### ✅ Do's
- Use gold and teal together (complementary)
- Apply glow effects to key elements
- Maintain aspect ratio of logo
- Use circle/eye as primary focal point
- Incorporate geometric patterns
- Layer transparency for depth
- Use zodiac symbols for cosmic feel

### ❌ Don'ts
- Don't use the logo on busy backgrounds
- Don't distort or rotate the logo
- Don't change color palette drastically
- Don't remove the eye symbol
- Don't make it too literal (not just theater)
- Don't oversaturate the design
- Don't forget the mystical/cosmic element

## Accessibility

### Color Contrast
- Gold text on teal background: ✅ WCAG AA
- White text on deep teal background: ✅ WCAG AAA
- Teal text on white background: ✅ WCAG AA

### Icon Usage
- Always pair icons with text labels
- Use descriptive alt text for images
- Ensure sufficient size (minimum 44x44px for touch targets)

### Typography
- Minimum font size: 14px (body text)
- Minimum contrast ratio: 4.5:1 (text)
- Line height: 1.5+ (readability)
